About the project

This app's purpose is to create the ideal calculator for you. We designed this app so that anyone, even with a little experience, could have a powerful tool in their hands.

We are two Computer Science students at the University of NC at Chapel Hill. From our experience in a lot of math classes. We wanted to create a software that was easy for math students to use and easy to use from anywhere at any time.

We hope to expand this project in the future and make it more accessible/better for everyone.
